About Derby City Raiders Booster Club

The Official Oakland Raiders Booster Club in Louisville, KY

Derby City Raiders was established in June 2012, we are a community and family oriented club full of die hard fans of the Oakland Raiders.

We love to get together to watch Raider Football games during the season, host parties and participate in fundraisers in the offseason. Although we root for a California NFL team, nearly all of our charitable efforts and dedication are channeled towards the community in the Kentuckiana area.

We have an amazing cast of cabinet members who constantly look for different ways to improve our club and increase the clubâ€™s popularity by recruiting new members, providing discounts at local Louisville area.

Most Oakland Raiders football games are not on regular broadcast television in the Louisville area. So, whether you live in the area or you are coming in from out of town, please come on out and join us on Raiders game day or any other special events that we may have. Everyone is welcome to the family!

We are a non-profit organization whose 3 main goals are to:

  • Engage with the community.
  • Grow the Derby City Raiders club.
  • Celebrate Raiders game days as a family.
